The great migration from centralized exchanges continues, and Base network is emerging as a primary beneficiary.

Over the past 24 hours, Nansen has tracked more than $2.3 billion in outflows from major centralized exchanges, with a significant portion of these funds bridging directly to Coinbase's Layer 2 network, Base.

The trend reflects growing unease among crypto holders following a series of exchange-related controversies over the past year. "People are finally taking self-custody seriously," observed one DeFi researcher. "And they're choosing Base because of its low fees and Coinbase's backing."
